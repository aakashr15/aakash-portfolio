More about me

Welcome to my portfolio! My name is Aakash, and I'm a multi-talented individual with a passion for programming, design, photography, and music.

As a recent graduate in mechanical engineering, I found my true calling in programming and graphic design during the COVID lockdown. I spent my time researching programming languages and technologies, and I quickly fell in love with the endless possibilities that they offer. As a result, I pursued my interest in programming and graphic design, and now I'm proud to call myself a developer and graphic designer.

In addition to my digital skills, I'm also a photographer and a pianist at my church. I believe that creativity and art are essential in all aspects of life, and I always strive to incorporate these elements into my work.

As a 2022 graduate fresher, I'm excited to pursue my career as a freelancer and use my skills to help others achieve their goals. I've already had the pleasure of creating websites and logos for several organizations, and I'm eager to expand my portfolio and work with even more clients.

I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow. I'm currently seeking an opportunity to work with an organization where I can contribute my skills and knowledge to help achieve their goals while continuing to grow myself professionally. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
